About Indo American Sr. Sec. School

Shaping young minds for a brighter tomorrow through knowledge, character, and innovation.

Our Legacy of Excellence

Founded in **1995**, Indo American Sr. Sec. School began with a singular vision: to be a beacon of quality, holistic education in Jhajjar. Over the decades, we have grown from a small institution into a leading CBSE-affiliated school, consistently setting benchmarks in academic and co-curricular achievements.

Our journey is marked by continuous adaptation. We were one of the first schools in the region to integrate **digital classrooms** and dedicated **robotics labs**, ensuring our students are prepared for the future economy. Every year, we celebrate a growing list of successful alumni placed in prestigious universities across India and abroad.
