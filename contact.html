Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about our school, admissions, and facilities.

What are the admission timings?

Admissions are open throughout the year, but the main session application deadline is March 15th. We recommend applying early for the academic session starting in April.

How can I schedule a school tour?

You can schedule a private tour by calling us or filling out the contact form above with the subject "School Tour Request." We conduct tours on weekdays between 9:00 AM and 1:00 PM.

What documents are required for admission?

Required documents include the child's birth certificate, previous school records (T.C. and report card), passport-size photos, and parent/guardian address proof.

Do you provide transportation?

Yes, we provide safe and reliable school bus services covering major areas around Jhajjar and surrounding towns. Routes and timings can be obtained from the Admin Office.
