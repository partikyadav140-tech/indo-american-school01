The Three-Step Admission Process

Indo American Sr. Sec. School welcomes students from all backgrounds. Our transparent process ensures every application is assessed fairly on merit and potential.

  1. Step 1: Online Application Submission
    Fill the comprehensive admission form below with accurate details. You will receive an acknowledgment receipt.
  2. Step 2: Interaction/Assessment (Aptitude & Counselling)
    Applicants will be invited for a mandatory interaction and a written assessment, specific to the grade level applying for.
  3. Step 3: Confirmation & Fee Submission
    Successful candidates will be notified. Admission is confirmed upon submission of required documents and payment of the first fee installment.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age requirements as per CBSE guidelines for the respective grade.
  • Original Transfer Certificate (T.C.) from the previous school (Grade 2 onwards).
  • A minimum B grade or equivalent in core subjects for Senior Secondary admission.
  • Successful completion of the school's entrance assessment/interview.

Required Documents

Birth Certificate, Address Proof, Aadhar Card, Last Report Card, and 4 Passport Size Photos.
