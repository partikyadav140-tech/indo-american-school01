Curriculum Overview: Stages of Learning

Pre-Primary (Nursery - KG)

Focus on foundational skills, sensory development, phonetics, and early numeracy through play-based learning methods.

Primary School (Grades 1 - 5)

Introduction to core subjects, digital literacy, critical thinking, and a strong emphasis on reading and writing skills.

Middle School (Grades 6 - 8)

Exploring complex concepts in Science, Mathematics, and Social Science; promoting inquiry-based learning and project work.

Senior School (Grades 9 - 12)

Rigorous board exam preparation, specialized subject streams, and career counselling for higher education preparedness.

Senior Secondary Streams Offered (Grade 11 & 12)

Our Focus on STEM & Robotics

In a world driven by technology, we place a high emphasis on **Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM)**. Our curriculum integrates hands-on learning through dedicated **ATAL Tinkering Labs (ATL)** and Robotics workshops.

  • Robotics Club: Weekly sessions for all middle school students.
  • Coding & AI: Mandatory modules on Python programming and Artificial Intelligence concepts (Grade 6 onwards).
  • Science Exhibitions: Annual platforms to showcase innovative projects and competitive spirit.
